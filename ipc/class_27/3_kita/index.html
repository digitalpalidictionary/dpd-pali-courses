    Kita

    Kita are a large group of suffixes which include present and past particles, as well as all the common noun and adjective suffixes.

    nta (prp)

    √añch añcha + nta añchanta turning; cutting
    √bhaṇ bhaṇa + nta bhaṇanta telling, preaching
    √ḍah ḍaha + nta ḍahanta burning
    √gal gaḷa + nta gaḷanta dripping, trickling
    √is iccha + nta icchanta wishing
    √jāgar jāgara + nta jāgaranta being awake
    √khād khāda + nta khādanta chewing
    √nad nada + nta nadanta shouting, roaring
    √pā piva + nta pivanta drinking
    ***
    ### āna (prp)
    √ādā ādiya + āna ādiyāna grasping
    √dhā daha + āna saddahāna believing
    √es esa + āna esāna seeking, searching
    √kam kāmaya + āna kāmayāna desiring
    √kar kubba + āna kubbāna doing
    √sas sasa + āna āsasāna wishing
    √sī saya + āna sayāna lying down
    √vad vada + āna vadāna speaking
    ***
    ### māna (prp)
    √as sa + māna samāna being
    √bhaj bhaja + māna bhajamāna associating
    √cu cava + māna cavamāna falling, dying
    √dā dadā + māna dadamāna giving
    √es esa + māna esamāna seeking
    √gajj gajja + māna gajjamāna roaring
    √kamp kampa + māna kampamāna shaking
    √man mañña + māna maññamāna imagining
    √ñā jānā + māna jānamāna knowing
    √tar tara + māna taramāna crossing, hurrying
    √vaḍḍh vaḍḍha + māna vaḍḍhamāna growing
    √yaj yaja + māna yajamāna sacrificing
    ***
    ### ta (pp)
    √bhañj + ta bhagga broken, shattered
    √caj + ta catta given up, abandoned
    √ḍah + ta daḍḍha burnt
    √gup + ta gutta protected
    √haṃs + ta haṭṭha elated
    √jhap + ta jhatta burnt out, tired
    √kas + ta kaṭṭha ploughed
    √kilis + ta kiliṭṭha soiled, dirtied
    √lup + ta lutta cut off
    √mad + ta matta intoxicated
    √nhā + ta nhāta bathed, washed
    √phus + ta phuṭṭha touched
    √raj + ta ratta dyed
    ***
    ### ita (pp)
    √citt + ita cittita designed, created
    √jaṭ + ita jaṭita twisted, tangled
    √kup + ita kupita angered, annoyed
    √luḷ + ita luḷita agitated, disturbed
    √nind + ita nindita blamed, criticised
    √pat + ita patita fallen
    √rus + ita rusita angered, irritated
    √sikh + ita sikkhita trained, practised
    √tajj + ita tajjita frightened, startled
    √tul + ita tulita weighed, measured
    √ujjh + ita ujjhita discarded, left
    √vas + ita vusita lived, completed
    √yāc + ita yācita asked for, begged
    ***
    ### na (pp)
    √bhid + na bhinna broken, damaged
    √car + na ciṇṇa done, practised
    √chad + na channa covered, hidden
    √chid + na chinna cut off, severed
    √dā + na dinna offered
    √hā + na hīna defeated, lost
    √kir + na vippakiṇṇa scattered about
    √lī + na patilīna retired, secluded
    √nud + na panuṇṇa driven out, sent away
    √pad + na uppanna born, arisen
    √sad + na sanna sunk, immersed
    √sad + na nisinna seated
    √tir + na nitthiṇṇa crossed over
    ***

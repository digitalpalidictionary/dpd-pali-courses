    + tar masculine agent nouns

    --- --- --- --- ---
    √ādā to take, seize + tar ādātar taker, who takes

    √bhid to break, split + tar bhettar breaker, divider
    √dis to see + tar dassetar guide
    √gam to go + tar gantar traveller, visitor

    √han to smite, kill + tar hantar striker, killer
    √khā to tell + tar akkhātar speaker, who tells
    √man to think + tar mantar thinker

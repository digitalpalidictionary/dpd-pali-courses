    • khattuṃ (times) adverbs of manner

    Indeclinable (abyayataddhita)

    khattuṃ (times) adverbs of manner
    anekasata + kkhattuṃ anekasatakkhattuṃ many 100’s of times
    cha + kkhattuṃ chakkhattuṃ 6 times
    chattiṃsā + kkhattuṃ chattiṃsakkhattuṃ 36 times
    dvatti + kkhattuṃ dvattikkhattuṃ 2 or 3 times
    dvisahassa + kkhattuṃ dvisahassakkhattuṃ 2000 times
    kati + kkhattuṃ katikkhattuṃ how many times?
    sahassa + kkhattuṃ sahassakkhattuṃ 1000 times
    satta + kkhattuṃ sattakkhattuṃ 7 times
    ti + kkhattuṃ tikkhattuṃ 3 times
    ***
    ### thā (in such way) adverbs of manner
    sabba + thā sabbathā in every way
    añña + thā aññathā in another way
    ta + thā tathā in that way
    ya + thā yathā in which way
    ubhaya + thā ubhayathā in both ways
    ***
    ### thaṃ (in such way) adverbs of manner
    ka + thaṃ kathaṃ in what way? how?
    ***
    ### ttha (in such place) adverbs of place
    añña + ttha aññattha in another place
    ima + ttha ettha in this place, here
    ima + ttha ittha in this place, here
    ka + ttha kattha in what place, where?
    ka + ttha kuttha in what place, where?
    para + ttha parattha in another place, elsewhere
    sabba + ttha sabbattha in every place, everywhere
    ta + ttha tattha in that place, there
    ya + ttha yattha in whichever place, wherever
    ***
    ### tra (in such place) adverbs of place
    amu + tra amutra in such place,
    añña + tra aññatra in another place, elsewhere
    ima + tra atra in this place, here
    ka + tra kutra in what place, where?
    sabba + tra sabbatra in every place, everywhere
    ta + tra tatra in that place, there
    ***
    ### dā & dāni (at such time) adverbs of time
    eka + dā ekadā at one time, one day
    ima + dāni idāni at this time, now
    ka + dā kadā at what time, when?
    ka + dā kudā at what time, when?
    sabba + dā sabbadā at all times, always
    ta + dā tadā at that time, then
    ya + dā yadā at whichever time, whenever
    ***
    ### dhā (ways, pieces) adverbs
    eka + dhā ekadhā in one way
    neka + dhā nekadhā in many ways
    dasa + dhā dasadhā in ten ways
    dvi + dhā dvedhā in two ways
    dvi + dhā dvidhā in two ways
    ti + dhā tidhā in three ways
    pañca + dhā pañcadhā in five ways
    sata + dhā satadhā in hundred ways
    satta + dhā sattadhā in seven ways
    ***
    ### so (in ways, by) distributive adverbs
    bahu + so bahuso in many ways
    bila + so bilaso into portions
    pañca + so pañcaso in five ways
    puthu + so puthuso in diverse ways
    sabba + so sabbaso in every way
    ***
    ### so (according to) distributive adverbs
    āyatana + so āyatanaso according to senses
    dhātu + so dhātuso according to elements
    hetu + so hetuso according to cause
    ṭhāna + so ṭhānaso according to place
    yoni + so yoniso according to source
    ***
    ### to (as) adverbs
    anicca + to aniccato as impermanent
    asāra + to asārato as essenceless
    asubha + to asubhato as ugly
    atta + to attato as oneself
    vajja + to vajjato as a mistake
    dukkha + to dukkhato as suffering
    khema + to khemato as safety
    momuha + to momuhato as foolish, stupid
    sacca + to saccato as true
    suñña + to suññato as empty
    ***
    ### to (from, in) adverbs
    antara + to antarato from within
    dīgha + to dīghato in length
    dūra + to dūrato from far away
    eka + to ekato on one side, together
    ima + to etto from this, from here
    ima + to ito from this, from here
    ka + to kuto from where?
    pacchā + to pacchato from behind
    pari + to parito from all around, everywhere
    pura + to purato in front of
    ***
    ### haṃ (in such place) adverbs of place
    ka + haṃ kahaṃ in what place, where?
    ta + haṃ tahaṃ in that place, there
    ***
    ### hiṃ (in such place) adverbs of place
    ka + hiṃ kuhiṃ in what place, where?
    ta + hiṃ tahiṃ in that place, there
    ***

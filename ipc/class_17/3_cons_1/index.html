    Possibility \#1

    Possibility #1¶

    When a word or particle ends in a vowel,

    the following consonant is generally reduplicated.

    There are thousands of such examples.

    e.g. pañca + khanda > pañcakkhandha

    In Kaccāyana this is called (28) para dvebhāvo ṭhāne and

    (29) vagge ghosāghosānaṃ tatiyapaṭhamā.

    vowel + k

           
    apa kanta apakkanta pp. departed, left
    abhi kamati abhikkamati pr. comes out
    upa kilesa upakkilesa masc. impurity
    ava > o kanti okkanti fem. conception, coming down

    vowel + kh

           
    pañca khandha pañcakkhandha masc. five aggregates
    taṇhā khaya taṇhākkhaya masc. destruction of craving
    ni khitta nikkhitta pp. discarded
    pura khata purakkhata pp. preferred; lit. made in front
    vowel + g      
    a gahesi aggahesi aor. took, grabbed
    vi gaha viggaha masc. quarrel, argument
    catu guṇa catugguṇa adj. folded four times

    vowel + gh

           
    pa gharanta paggharanta prp. dripping
    sakuṇa ghi sakuṇagghi masc. hawk; lit. bird killer
    eka ghana ekagghana adj. dense; lit. one mass

    vowel + c

           
    na cuta accuta adj. everlasting
    pari caja pariccaja masc. boundary
    a + pari catta apariccatta pp. not given up

    vowel + ch

           
    vigata chanda vigatacchanda adj. free from desire
    seta chatta setacchatta nt. white umbrella

    vowel + j

           
    pa jalati pajjalati pr. burns
    puthu jana puthujjana masc. ordinary man

    vowel + jh

           
    pa jhāyati pajjhāyati pr. contemplates, thinks
    abhi jhā abhijjhā f. covetousness

    vowel + ñ

           
    sabba ñū sabbaññū adj. all knowing
    vi ñāṇa viññāṇa nt. consciousness

    vowel + ṭh

           
    anu ṭhita anuṭṭhita pp. established
    upa ṭhapeti upaṭṭhapeti pr. applies
    adhi ṭhāna adhiṭṭhāna nt. determination
    cira ṭhitika ciraṭṭhitika adj. long standing

    vowel + ḍ

           
    ni ḍeti niḍḍeti4 pr. weeds out


    (commentary example)

    vowel + t

           
    cha tiṃsati chattiṃsati fem. thirty-six
    catu tāla catuttāla adj. four palm trees in height
    pari yasati parittasati pr. worries

    vowel + th

           
    na thaddha atthaddha adj. not stubborn
    vi thāra vitthāra m. breadth
    anu thunāti anutthunāti pr. wails, moans
    o tharati ottharati pr. spreads over

    vowel + d

           
    a dasā addasā imperf. saw
    catu disā catuddisā fem. four directions

    vowel + dh

           
    anu dhaṃseti anuddhaṃseti pr. attacks
    sovaṇṇa dhaja sovaṇṇaddhaja adj. with golden flags

    vowel + n

           
    ku nadī kunnadī fem. stream

    vowel + p

           
    na pamāda appamāda m. carefulness
    ati pago atippago adv. very early
    āyu pamāṇa āyuppamāṇa nt. length of life

    vowel + ph

           
    pati pharati paṭippharati pr. blasts back
    pa phoṭetvā papphoṭetvā abs. having shaken out

    after a vowel, v can become bb

    vowel + v

           
    giri vaja giribbaja nt. mountain cave
    cha vassa chabbassa nt. six years

    vowel + bh

           
    a bhaya abbhaya adj. without fear
    puna > pono bhavika ponobbhavika adj. leading to repeated existence

    vowel + m

           
    ku magga kummagga masc. bad road; wrong way

    vowel + y

           
    se yathā’pi seyyathā’pi ind. just like

    vowel + l

           
    vijju latā vijjullatā fem. forks of lightning
    ava > o lujjati ullujjati pr. is breaking down

    vowel + s

           
    pa sasati passasati pr. exhales, breathes out
    diṭṭha suta diṭṭhassuta nt. what is seen and heard
    anu saritar anussaritar masc. who recollects

    Counter-examples:

    ajāta + sattu > ajātasattu

    upa + pajjati > upapajjati

    pa + dāleti > padāleti

    • Prefixes (upasagga)
    • ā-
      up to, upwards, towards, near to, at, backwards, away, all round, intensely, until, as far as[^15]
    • ati- acc-
      beyond, across, over, past, very much, very, excessively
    • adhi- ajjh-
      over, above, on, upon, at, to, in, great, superiority, excessively, completely, regarding, within; inside
    • anu-
      following, after, alongside, according to, near to, behind, below, less than, beneath, suitable for, repeatedly
    • apa-
      off, away, away from, badly, negatively, wrongly, forth, reverentially, less, down
    • api- pi- (rare)
      on, over, above, onto, close upon, cover
    • abhi- abbh-
      to, unto, towards, beyond, against, in the direction of, very, reverently, completely, particularity, highly, strongly
    • ava- o-
      down, off, away, back, aside, inside, less, disrespect
    • ud-
      upwards, above, up, forth, off, out, wrong, intensely
    • upa-
      unto, to, close, towards, near to, with, by the side of, strongly, as, like, up to, up, higher, (opposed to apa)
    • ni-
      out, off, forth, down, back, into, downwards, in, under, without
    • nī-
      away from, off, out, removal
    • pa- (sk pra)
      forward, to, forth, fore, towards, up, over, inside, specifically, intensity, away, with, source, beginning
    • pati- paṭi- (sk prati)
      against, back to, return, oppositely, towards, personally, similarly, near
    • parā- (quite rare)
      beyond, again, continuously, wrongly, away, back, opposite, aside
    • pari- pali-
      around, all around, about, all about, overall, completely
    • vi-
      apart, away, without, separately, variously, special, distinctly, dispersion, opposite, transformation
    • saṃ- sā- (sk sam)
      with, along, together, well, fully, perfectly, intensely, by oneself, again

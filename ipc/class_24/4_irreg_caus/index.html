    Irregular Causative Bases

    √jan to be born *e jane janeti generates
    √ādā to take *āpe ādape samādapeti arouses
    √ar to attain *āpe appe appeti reaches
    √car to walk *e chāre nicchāreti says
    √dā to be pure *āpe dape pariyodapeti purifies
    √dhā to place *āpe dahāpe samādahāpeti causes to light
    √dīp to shine *e limpe ālimpeti sets fire to
    √dis to see *e daṃse vidaṃseti displays
    √dis to see *e dasse dasseti presents
    √gah to grasp *āpe gahāpe uggahāpetvā having caused to take
    √hā to decrease *āpe jāpe jāpeti confiscates
    √har to carry *e hare āhareti brings
    √kam to go *e khāme nikkhāmenta causing to go out
    √kar to do *e khāre abhisaṅkhāreti performs
    √ñā to know *āpe ṇāpe āṇāpeti orders; directs
    √nadh to wrap *āpe nandhāpe pilandhāpetvā having caused to adorn
    √nam to bend *e ṇāme pariṇāmeti allocates
    √nas to be lost *e nessa nessati will destroy
    √phus to touch *e phasse phasseti touches
    √plu to float *āpe pilāpe opilāpeti immerses
    √ram to delight *e rame rameti gives delight
    √ruh to ascend *e rope āropeti makes climb up
    √sad to sink, sit *āpe sīdāpe nisīdāpeti causes to sit down
    √sam to be calm *e same vūpasameti settles
    √siv to warm *e sivve visibbeti warms
    √snih to love *e sinehe sinehita attractive;
    √ṭhā to stand *āpe ṭhapāpe ṭhapāpetvā having caused to stand
    √vā to blow *āpe bāpe nibbāpeti makes cool down
    √vac to say *e pāce vipāceti spreads about
    √vaj to go on *aya bājaya pabbājayanta banishing
    √vaj to go on *e bāje pabbājeti drives away
    √var to cover *e rupe pārupeti wraps
    √vatt to proceed *e batte abhinibbatteti produces
    √vij to shake *e beje ubbejetar agitator
    √vīj to fan *aya bījaya bījayamāna fanning
    √yat to arrange *āpe yādāpe paṭiyādāpetvā having organised

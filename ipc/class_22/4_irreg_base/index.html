    Irregular Verbal Bases

    Most verbs in Pāli are quite predictable in their formation, but there are a few irregularities. Here follows a list of a few common ones. You will be quite familiar with many of these by now, but some will seem strange.

    Irregular Present Tense Bases

    √as sa santi there are
    √chand chāde chādeti pleases; gives pleasure
    √ḍaṃs ḍasa ḍasati bites, stings
    √dā de deti gives; donates
    √dhā daha dahati considers as; takes as
    √dis passa passati sees
    √dis dakkha dakkhati sees
    √gam gaccha gacchati goes
    √is iccha icchati wishes
    √jan jāya jāyati is born
    √kar kubba kubbati does; makes
    √kar kuru kurute one oneself does
    √khī khiyya khiyyati criticises; complains
    √ñā jānā jānāti knows
    √pā piva pivati drinks
    √rabh rambha ārambhati begins
    √sad sīda sīdati sinks
    √ṭhā tiṭṭha tiṭṭhati stands
    √vad vade vadeti speaks
    √var pura pāpurati covers, wraps with
    √vid binda nibbindati is dis-enchanted
    √yam veccha anuppavecchati gives, supports
    ***

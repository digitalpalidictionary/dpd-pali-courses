# Product Guidelines

## Content & Prose Style
- **Scholarly Precision:** Maintain high standards of grammatical and philological accuracy in line with DPD's reputation.
- **Accessible Support:** Use clear, supportive English to guide learners through complex Pāḷi concepts.
- **Traditional Respect:** Integrate traditional Buddhist terminology and maintain a respectful tone toward the source texts.
- **Consistency:** Ensure Pāḷi terms and grammatical explanations match the terminology used in the Digital Pāli Dictionary.

## Visual Identity & UX
- **DPD Ecosystem Alignment:** The visual style must mirror the established DPD aesthetic (as seen on `dpdict.net`).
- **Color Palette & Typography:** Use the colors and fonts defined in the DPD identity (e.g., specific shades of red/maroon and clean, readable serifs/sans-serifs for Pāḷi diacritics).
- **Readability First:** Prioritize clear typography and spacing to facilitate intensive language study and reading.
- **Navigation:** Maintain a structured, intuitive hierarchy consistent with DPD documentation styles.

## Technical Standards
- **Markdown Integrity:** Source files should use standard Markdown with consistent use of diacritics for Pāḷi words.
- **Asset Management:** Follow the structure observed in `website_example/` for shared data and identity assets.
- **Responsive Design:** The generated HTML must be mobile-friendly and accessible across devices.

**In Summary**


| Pāli | POS | Grammar |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Nominative | naro | the man (subject) |
| 2. Accusative | naraṃ | the man (object) |
| 3. Instrumental | narena | by, with, through the man |
| 4. Dative | narāya, narassa | to the man, for the man |
| 5. Ablative | narā, naramhā, narasmā | from the man |
| 6. Genitive | narassa | of the man, the man’s |
| 7. Locative | nare, naramhi, narasmiṃ | in, on, at the man |
| 8. Vocative | nara, narā | hey, man! o, man! |

4\. SN 12.15 kaccānagotta-suttaṃ

“‘sammādiṭṭhi sammādiṭṭhī’ti, bhante, vuccati. kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, sammādiṭṭhi hotī”ti?

“dvayanissito kho ayaṃ, kaccāna, loko yebhuyyena — atthitaṃ ca eva n’atthitaṃ ca. lokasamudayaṃ kho, kaccāna, yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya passato yā loke n’atthitā sā na hoti. lokanirodhaṃ kho, kaccāna, yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya passato yā loke atthitā sā na hoti. upaya-upādāna-abhinivesa-vinibandho kho ayaṃ, kaccāna, loko yebhuyyena. taṃ ce ayaṃ upayupādānaṃ cetaso adhiṭṭhānaṃ abhinivesa-anusayaṃ na upeti na upādiyati na adhiṭṭhāti — ‘attā me’ti. ‘dukkhaṃ eva uppajjamānaṃ uppajjati, dukkhaṃ nirujjhamānaṃ nirujjhatī’ti na kaṅkhati na vicikicchati aparapaccayā ñāṇaṃ eva assa ettha hoti. ettāvatā kho, kaccāna, sammādiṭṭhi hoti.

“‘sabbaṃ atthī’ti kho, kaccāna, ayaṃ eko anto. ‘sabbaṃ n’atthī’ti ayaṃ dutiyo anto. ete te, kaccāna, ubho ante anupagamma majjhena tathāgato dhammaṃ deseti.

# Review

1.  yo ariyasaccāni avecca passati.
***
*[SNP13](https://find.dhamma.gift/bw/snp/snp2.1.html) ratana sutta*
***
2.  kacci nu kho ahaṃ suññ’āgāre abhiramāmi iti
***
*[AN10.48](https://find.dhamma.gift/bw/an/an10.48.html) pabbajitābhiṇha sutta*
***
3.  yathā parisā alaṃ dassanāya gantuṃ, tathā ayaṃ bhikkhusaṅgho.
***
*[AN4.190](https://find.dhamma.gift/bw/an/an4.190.html) uposatha sutta (simpl)*
***
4.  abhabbo eva tāva kālaṃ kātuṃ yāva na sotāpattiphalaṃ sacchikaroti.
***
*[SN25.1](https://find.dhamma.gift/bw/sn/sn25.1.html) cakkhu sutta*
***
5.  yasmā ca kho, ānanda, suññaṃ attena vā attaniyena vā tasmā suñño loko iti vuccati

    *[SN35.85](https://find.dhamma.gift/bw/sn/sn35.85.html) suññataloka sutta*
***
6.  yena eva bhagavā tena eva ahaṃ assaṃ
***
*[SN55.23](https://find.dhamma.gift/bw/sn/sn55.23.html) godhasakkasutta*
***

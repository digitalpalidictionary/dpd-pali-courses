# Root Group 3 – divādigaṇa
***
= div + ādi + gaṇa
***
= √div + etc + group
***
This common group has the conjugational sign **ya** which is added to the root.
***
√div (shine) + ya \> divya \> di**bb**a
***
dibba + ti \> dibbati (he shines, illuminates)
***

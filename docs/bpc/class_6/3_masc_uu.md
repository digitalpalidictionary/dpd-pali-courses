# Masculine nouns ending in -ū
***
This is a small class of nouns, mostly formed from the **ññū** (knower) and **gū** (goer) ; e.g. *matta***ññū** (knower of the limit), *anta***gū** (one who has gone to the end)
***
Many of these nouns can also be used as adjectives.
***

| 1.nom | vid**ū** | vid**ū** <br>vid**uno** <br>vid**ūno** |
|---|---|---|
| 2.acc | vid**uṃ** | vid**ū** <br>vid**uno** <br>vid**ūno** |
| 3.inst | vid**unā** | vid**ūhi** |
| 4.dat | vid**uno** | vid**ūnaṃ** |
| 5.abl | vid**unā** | vid**ūhi** |
| 6.gen | vid**uno** <br>vid**ussa** | vid**ūnaṃ** |
| 7.loc | vid**umhi** <br>vid**usmiṃ** | vid**ūsu** |
| 8.voc | vid**ū** | vid**ū** <br>vid**uno** |

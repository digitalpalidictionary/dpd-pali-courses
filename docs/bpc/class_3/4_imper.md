# Imperative Verbs!

The imperative mood can express a command, gentle advice, a blessing, a curse, a wish or a prayer.

***

e.g. gacch**a**! (Go! You must go!)

***

phāsu vihar**atu** (Let him live comfortably!)

***

vassasataṃ jīv**a** (May you live 100 years!)

***

***

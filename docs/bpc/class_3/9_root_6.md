

### Root Group 6 - gahādigaṇa

= gah + ādi + gaṇa

= √gah + etc + group

It has the conjugational sign **ṇhā.**

√gah (grasp) + **ṇhā** \> gaṇhā

gaṇhā + ti \> gaṇhāti (he grabs, takes)

***

***

√tas (thirst) + **ṇhā** \> taṇhā

taṇhā + ā \> taṇhā (craving)

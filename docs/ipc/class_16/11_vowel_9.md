# Possibility \#9

When a word ends in a **long vowel** and the next word begins with **e**,
***
**e** may be changed to **ri** and the long vowel **shortened**.
***
yath**ā**![](media/600cdd2c0fa42269d4d2097fe97305d7.png)**e**va \> yatha**ri**va
***
**ā![](media/600cdd2c0fa42269d4d2097fe97305d7.png)e \> ari**
***
This means:
***
when a word ends in **ā**,
***
and the next word starts with **e**,
***
**ā** is shortened
***
and **e** becomes **ri**
***
In Kaccāyana this is called (22) **ev’ādissa ri pubbo ca rasso.**
***
*sara-sandhi-niṭṭhitaṃ*
***

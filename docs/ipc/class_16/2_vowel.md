# Vowel Sandhi *(sara-sandhi)*
***
Vowel Sandhi occurs when the vowel at the end of one part of speech meets the vowel at the beginning of another part of speech.
***
na + atthi \> ?
***
paññā + indriya \> ?
***
cakkhu + indriya \> ?
***
The basic possibilities of Vowel Sandhi are :
***
1.  **Elision**, *lopa,* a vowel gets dropped or elided.
***
2.  **Lengthening**, *dīghaṃ,* a short vowel becomes long.
***
3.  **Change**, *ādesa*, a new vowel gets formed.
***
4.  **Insertion**, *āgama*, a consonant is inserted between two vowels.

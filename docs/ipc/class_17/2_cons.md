
# Consonant Sandhi (*vya√±jana-sandhi)*

***

Consonant Sandhi occurs when a word **ending in a vowel** is followed by a word **beginning with a consonant**. (or parts of a word)

***

***

There are three basic types of Consonant Sandhi

***

***

1.  Re-duplication

***

2.  Shortening

***

3.  Lengthening

***

***

Re-duplication and shortening occur commonly in prose, while shortening and lengthening often occur for the sake of meter in verse.

***

# Possibility \#8
***
When **ṃ** is followed by **a consonant**,
***
**ṃ** may be **elided**.
***
in verses
***
e.g. buddhāna**ṃ** + **s**āsanaṃ \> buddhān**a**sāsanaṃ
***
In Kaccāyana this is called (39) **byañjane ca**
***
**ṃ elided before a consonant**

|---|---|---|---|
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| buddhāna**ṃ** | **s**āsanaṃ | buddhān**a**sāsanaṃ | buddha’s teaching |
| ariya-<br>saccāna**ṃ** | **d**assanaṃ | ariyasaccān**a** dassanaṃ | seeing the noble truths |
| paresa**ṃ** | **p**āva | pares**a** pāva | tells to others |
| vanasmi**ṃ** | **v**ānaro | vanasm**i** vānaro | monkey in the forest |
| pajaheyyu**ṃ** | **d**ukkhaṃ | pajaheyy**u** dukkhaṃ | they could abandon suffering |

***
**Counter-examples:**
***
all the normal cases where **ṃ** is not elided
***

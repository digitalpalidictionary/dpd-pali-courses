# Possibility \#10
***
A **ṃ** may be inserted **before** a **vowel** or a **consonant**.
***
The **ṃ** then **may** change according to **Possibility \#2**.
***
e.g. ava + siro \> ava**ṃ**siro
***
In Kaccāyana this is called (37) **niggahitañ’ca.**
***
| **ṃ insertion** |             |                  |                  |
|-----------------|-------------|------------------|------------------|
|                 |             |                  |                  |
| av**a**         | **s**iro    | ava**ṃ**siro     | adj. head-first  |
| an**u**         | **th**ūlāni | anu**ṃ** thūlāni | subtle and gross |
| yāv**a**        | **c**a idha | yāva**ñ**c’idha  | and as long as   |
***
**Counter-examples:**
***
idha + eva \> idheva
***
pecca + sagge \> pecca sagge
***

# Possibility \#3
***
When **ṃ** is followed by **l**,
***
**ṃ** can become **l**.
***
e.g. sa**ṃ** + **l**ekha \> sa**ll**ekha
***
This rule is not found in Kaccāyana, but can be found in Rūpasiddhi under (49) **vagg’antaṃ** **vā vagge**.
***
**ṃ + l \> ll**

|---|---|---|---|
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| sa**ṃ** | **l**apati | sa**ll**apati | pr. chats together |
| sa**ṃ** | **l**ekha | sa**l**lekha | m. erasing, effacing |
| paṭi + sa**ṃ** | **l**īna | paṭisa**l**līna | pp. secluded |
| pu**ṃ** | **l**iṅga | pu**l**liṅga | m. masculine gender |

# Possibility \#7
***
When **ṃ** is followed by **a vowel**,
***
**ṃ** may be **elided**.
***
In this case, the vowel may be **lengthened**.
***
e.g. im**aṃ** + **a**haṃ \> im**ā**haṃ
***
In Kaccāyana this is called (38) **kvaci lopaṃ**
***
and (15) **dīghaṃ**.
***
**ṃ elided before a vowel + vowel lengthened**
| | | | |
|---|---|---|---|
| ima**ṃ**                                       | **a**haṃ | im**ā**haṃ         | this I      |
| eka**ṃ i**da**ṃ**                              | **a**haṃ | eka**m**id**ā**haṃ | this one I  |
| eva**ṃ**                                       | **a**haṃ | ev**ā**haṃ         | like this I |
***
**Counter-examples:**
***
aha**ṃ e**va \> aha**ṃ** eva
***
eta**ṃ a**tthaṃ \> eta**ṃ** atthaṃ
***

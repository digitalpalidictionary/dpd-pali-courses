# Possibility \#4
***
When **ṃ** is followed by **e,** it can become **ññ**
***
When **ṃ** is followed by **h,** it can become **ñ**
***
e.g. ta**ṃ** + **e**va \> ta**ññ**eva
***
aya**ṃ** + **h**i \> aya**ñ**hi
***
In Kaccāyana this is called (32) **ehe ñaṃ.**
***
| | | | |
|---|---|---|---|
| ta**ṃ**       | **e**va | ta**ññ**eva       | just that       |
| aha**ṃ**      | **e**va | aha**ññ**eva      | just I          |
| paccatta**ṃ** | **e**va | paccatta**ññ**eva | just personally |
| aya**ṃ**      | **h**i  | aya**ñ**hi        | this indeed     |
| eva**ṃ**      | **h**i  | eva**ñ**hi        | thus indeed     |
| ki**ṃ**       | **h**i  | ki**ñ**hi         | what indeed?    |
***
**Counter-examples:**
***
eva**ṃ e**taṃ \> eva**ṃ** etaṃ
***
eva**ṃ h**oti \> eva**ṃ** hoti
***


### Possibility \#5

***

When **ṃ** is followed by **y,** they can be assimilated into **ññ**

***

***

e.g. sa**ṃ** + **y**ojana \> sa**ññ**ojana

***

***

In Kaccāyana this is called (33) **sa ye ca.**

***

***

| **ṃ + y \> ññ** |             |                |                       |
|-----------------|-------------|----------------|-----------------------|
| ya**ṃ**         | **y**aṃ eva | ya**ññ**adeva  | whatever it is        |
| sa**ṃ**         | **y**ata    | sa**ññ**ata    | pp. self-controlled   |
| sa**ṃ**         | **y**oga    | sa**ññ**oga    | m. bond, tie          |
| visa**ṃ**       | **y**utta   | visa**ññ**utta | pp. detached, unyoked |

***

***

**Counter-example:**

***

saṃ + yoga \> saṃyoga

***

anu + saṃ + yāyitvā \> anusaṃyāyitvā

***

saṃ + yutta \> saṃyutta

***

imasmiṃ + yeva \> imasmiṃyeva

***


# Phonetic changes of -t-

***

This occurs with the **past participle** or **adjectives** ending -ta,

***

**infinitive** -tuṃ, **absolutive** -tvā, **masculine nouns** ending in -tar,

***

**neuter nouns ending** in -ta, **feminine nouns** ending in -ti, etc

***

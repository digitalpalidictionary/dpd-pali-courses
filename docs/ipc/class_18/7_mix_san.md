# Mixed Sandhi (vomissaka-sandhi)
***
| **di \> dy \> jj**       | ya**di** evaṃ             | ya**jj**evaṃ       |
|--------------------------|---------------------------|--------------------|
| **di \> dy \> jj**       | sa √div \> dyu \> **jj**u | sa**jj**u          |
| **dhi \> dhy \> jjh**    | bo**dhi** aṅga            | bo**jjh**aṅga      |
| **dhi \> dhy \> jjh**    | a**dhi** āvasati          | a**jjh**āvasati    |
| **bhi \> bhy \> bbh**    | a**bhi** uggacchati       | a**bbh**uggacchati |
| **bhi \> bhy \> bbh**    | a**bhi** ācikkhati        | a**bbh**ācikkhati  |
| **pi \> py \> pp**       | a**pi** ekacce            | a**pp**ekacce      |
| **pi \> py \> pp**       | a**pi** ekadā             | a**pp**ekadā       |
| **ri \> li \> ly \> ll** | pa**ri** aṅka             | pa**ll**aṅka       |
| **ri \> li \> ly \> ll** | vi pa**ri** √as           | vipa**ll**āsa      |
| **ti \> ty \> cc**       | a**ti** **e**ti           | a**cc**eti         |
| **ti \> ty \> cc**       | a**ti** **u**ṇha          | a**cc**uṇha        |

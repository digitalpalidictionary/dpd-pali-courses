# Imperative (*pañcamī*)
***
Kaccāyana 415. āṇaty’āsiṭṭhe’nuttakāle pañcamī.
***
“When a command, a wish, an unspecified time, imperative”
***
Please conjugate the word **gacchati** in the imperative active voice.
***
Let’s look at the imperative **reflexive** **voice** and **passive voice.**
***
(Theory) How is the reflexive formed? How is the passive formed?
***
**Imperative Attanopada “oneself must train”<br>(√sikkh + a \> sikkha)**

|---|---|---|
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | **sg** | **pl** |
| 3 paṭhama | sikkh**ataṃ** | sikkh**antaṃ** |
| 2 majjhima | sikkh**assu** | sikkh**avho** |
| 1 uttama | sikkh**e** | sikkh**āmase** |

***
**Imperative Passive Attanopada “must be bound (by oneself)”<br>(√badh + ya \> bajjha)**

|---|---|---|
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | **sg** | **pl** |
| 3 paṭhama | bajjh**ataṃ** | bajjh**antaṃ** |
| 2 majjhima | bajjh**assu** | bajjh**avho** |

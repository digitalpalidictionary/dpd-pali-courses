# Present Tense (*vattamānā*)
***
Kaccāyana 414. vattamānā paccuppanne.
***
“present tense when present-time”
***
Please conjugate the word **gacchati** in the present tense active voice (*parassapada)*.
***
Let’s look at the present tense **reflexive** **voice** (*attanopada)* and **passive voice**.
***
(Theory) How is the reflexive formed? How is the passive formed?
***
**Present Tense Attanopada “kills by oneself” <br>√han + a \> hana**

|---|---|---|
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | **s** | **pl** |
| 3 paṭhama | hana**te** | hanan**te**<br>hana**re** |
| 2 majjhima | hana**se** | hana**vhe** |
| 1 uttama | han**e** | han**āmhe** |

***
**Present Tense Passive Attanopada √han “is killed (by oneself)[^10]”<br>√han + ya \> hañña**

|---|---|---|
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | **s** | **pl** |
| 3 paṭhama | hañña**te** | hañña**nte**<br>hañña**re** |
| 2 majjhima | hañña**se** | hañña**vhe** |
| 1 uttama | haññ**e** | haññ**āmhe** |

***
[^10]: Please note that very often reflexive can be translated without this reflexive pronoun
